About Me

As a 2nd-Year Law, Criminal Justice, and Society Major at the University of Pittsburgh, I aspire to develop a professional, yet reliable relationship between clients and attorneys for the collective resolution of fair judgement and equal peace. My passionate leadership, clear communication skills, and detail-oriented mindset formulate an efficient and united legal workforce.

Field of Interest

Upon graduation in Fall 2027, I intend to attend law school for the study of criminal trial or family law. Becoming a part of a criminal or family law firm remains the end goal.

Diversity Statement

As a woman of color going into the legal field, I hold high value in diversity in and out of the workplace. I am aware that making just decisions means removing any potential bias, however this does not mean I overlook any injustices in race, gender, age, and other categories alike. I foster a work environment that prioritizes morality and equality for the care of all individuals in which I interact. I stand on the belief that without diversity in a legal workplace, true justice cannot be reached.
